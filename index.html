Lemon Bars

Spongy lemon cake with a tangy and sweet lemon juice glaze.
Give chocolate a break with this citrus treat!

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 20-25 mins

Ingredient List

For the cake:

  • 1/2 cup of butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • Zest of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp of fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

For the glaze:

  • 3/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1-1/2 tbsp of lemon juice
  • Zest of 1/2 lemon

Preparation and baking:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 fahrenheit.
  2. Grease an 8"x8" baking dish.
  3. Beat softened butter, sugar, and lemon zest until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs in 1 at a time until incorporated.
  5. Add lemon juice.
  6. Add flour and baking powder and mix until smooth.
  7. Place in oven and bake for 20-25 mins.
  8. While baking, add ingredients for glaze into a bowl and mix until smooth.
  9. Remove cake from oven and let cool for 15 mins.
  10. Poke holes throughout the top of the cake and pour the glaze over the cake.
  11. Cut into squares and serve immediately or when cooled.
